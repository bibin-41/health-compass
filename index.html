Health Compass

Your personal dashboard for vital metrics

BMI Calculator

Check your Body Mass Index based on your height and weight to see your weight category.

Calorie Calculator

Determine your daily calorie requirements based on your activity level and body metrics.

Body Fat %

Accurately estimate your body composition using the US Navy tape measure method.

Hydration Needs

Calculate your optimal daily water intake based on your body weight and lifestyle.

Macronutrient Split

Break down your daily calorie target into exact grams of carbs, protein, and fat.

Cholesterol Profile

Analyze your lipid panel including Total, LDL, HDL, and Triglycerides.

Oxygen (SpO2)

Check your blood oxygen levels against standard clinical thresholds.

Hemoglobin Levels

Check your blood oxygen-carrying capacity against standard clinical ranges.

Blood Sugar

Enter your fasting or post-meal glucose levels to verify if they fall within a normal range.

Blood Pressure

Input your systolic and diastolic numbers to check your cardiovascular health status.

Protein Calculator

Discover your optimal daily protein intake based on your body weight and fitness goals.

Sleep Calculator

Determine your optimal nightly sleep range based on your age and physical activity level.

About HealthCompass

HealthCompass is an all-in-one personal wellness dashboard developed as the Idea Lab project by Group 5 (S2 Computer Science and Engineering) at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering. It bridges the gap between clinical health tracking and daily fitness goals by providing instant, data-driven insights based on standard medical guidelines. Calculate your metrics, understand your baseline, and navigate your fitness journey.
